Our curriculum allows you to check the content of your lessons before class and track your own progress. With the successful completion of each goal-oriented lesson, you will see progress in your Japanese ability. Also, you will achieve a deeper understanding of Japanese culture. As a benchmark for passing each unit test, you will receive a FUJI SCHOOL certificate. These certificates and tests will help to remind you of how much you have accomplished towards your goal of learning a new language. We are here to give you the most personalized language learning experience possible.

Please use the reception page to contact us with any questions about our school, curriculum, or teachers before enrolling at FUJI SCHOOL. We would be happy to help you choose the courses and lessons that are right for you. FUJI SCHOOL is run by a tight-nit group of professional educators who are passionate about teaching Japanese. Our goal is to provide you with the best mix of language education and cultural exchange. We are here for you!
